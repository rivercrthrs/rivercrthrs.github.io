Emotional Intelligence

Developing emotional intelligence is key to thriving in all parts of life, whether you’re working with a team to accomplish something or trying to make a important decision on your own. The basics concepts as illustrated in the book Search Inside Yourself are as follows:

  • - Self-Awareness
  • Knowing one’s internal states, preferences, resources, and intuitions.
  • - Self-Regulation
  • Ability to manage internal states, impulses, and emotions.
  • - Motivation
  • Understanding tendencies that facilitate reaching goals.
  • - Empathy
  • Awareness of others’ emotions and concerns,
  • - Social Skills
  • Adeptness at working with others
